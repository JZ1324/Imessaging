How it works

Three steps from chat.db to clarity.

Local processing, readable dashboards, exportable reports.

01

Grant access

Enable Full Disk Access so the app can read chat.db on your Mac.

Contacts access is optional.
02

Generate analytics

We compute reply speed, streaks, and top chats locally with trimmed averages.

No uploads. No background sync.
03

Export your report

Open the HTML summary or export a folder for archiving and sharing.

Auto refresh keeps it current.

Privacy

Your data stays yours.

All analytics run locally. Cloud sync is optional and only enabled after you sign in. You control when it syncs.

  • Local database access only when you grant Full Disk Access.
  • Contacts access is optional and used only to label chats.
  • Sync uses your own Supabase project.

Local first

Processing happens on your Mac. No upload step required.

Permission control

You decide when to grant Full Disk Access or Contacts access.

Optional sync

Supabase sync is off by default and uses your own credentials.

Where is the data stored?

All local analytics read from chat.db on your Mac. Reports are exported to your chosen folder.

Do I need an account?

No account is required for local analytics. Supabase sync is optional.

What permissions are required?

Full Disk Access is required to read chat.db. Contacts access is optional.

How do updates work?

Updates use Sparkle and are downloaded directly from the appcast feed.

Can I remove the app cleanly?

Yes. Delete the app and exported reports to remove local artifacts.

Does the demo use my data?

The live demo uses a synthetic dataset for illustration only.
